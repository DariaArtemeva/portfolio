VR Game "Electronics around us"

"Electronics Around Us" - a free educational VR game for elementary school students, focused on discovering Ohm’s Law through hands-on experiments.

Guided by a friendly robot Sparky in the futuristic city of Elektrocity, players explore three interactive levels:

  • Experiment with resistors and batteries to control LED brightness
  • Shrink down to fix a broken lightbulb circuit
  • Repair airplane wing lights by correctly connecting wires

Built in Unity with 3D assets created in Blender, fully compatible with HTC Vive

3D Game "Lesný Duch: Návrat"

Watch the trailer on YouTube

"Lesný Duch: Návrat" - atmospheric first-person survival horror / interactive movie built in Unity for Windows. Inspired by the Slovak cult video “Lesný duch” (2002), the game focuses on tension, cinematic pacing, and story-driven exploration. Players move through a dark forest, interact with objects (F), survive QTE sequences, and experience Timeline-driven cutscenes with voiced moments (ElevenLabs) and layered ambient sound design (forest, fire, footsteps, distant shouts).

Features:

  • First-person movement + camera control (WASD + mouse)
  • Object interaction system (F)
  • QTE system (timed input events)
  • Cinematic cutscenes (Unity Timeline)
  • Ambient horror audio + voice narration (ElevenLabs)

3D Game "Ball game"

"Ball Game" - exhilarating 3D platformer rolling a metal ball across narrow wooden platforms high in a vast forest canopy.

Safely navigate to the finish, collect nuts for star-rated scores, and survive falls or spider attacks with 5 lives—respawning at tree stump checkpoints!

Tackle dynamic obstacles:

  • Mushroom trampolines to leap gaps
  • Swinging hammers knocking you off
  • Shrinking/enlarging platforms altering ball size & control
  • Gusty fans blowing you away
  • Deadly spiders draining lives
  • Precision block walls

3D Game "Neptune’s Echo"

"Neptune’s Echo" - nostalgic PS1-inspired 3D roll-a-ball adventure in a haunting underwater labyrinth.

Guide your metal ball through 5 unique maze levels and collect every yellow pickup cube to win.

Watch your lives: start with 5 lives and lose one on contact with enemies-especially the relentless patrolling shark overhead.

Unlock paths with keys & doors, race against a timer, and use speed boosters to slip past danger.

Enemies have distinct behaviors: the shark hunts, the crab patrols back-and-forth, and a teleporting gnome guards boosters.

Immerse in retro aesthetics:

  • Low-poly textures & post-processing effects
  • Pixelated fonts & chiptune music
  • Main menu + immersive game scene

Product Visualization "Dan Wesson Magnum 375"

An interactive Unity product visualization showcasing a detailed 3D revolver model with studio-style lighting and a clean presentation focus.

Key features:

  • Free 3D inspection: rotate / zoom / view the model from any angle (editor-like control)
  • URP-based rendering with optimized lighting: bright subject + dark background for strong contrast
  • Timeline animation demonstrating the movement of selected mechanical parts
  • Boundary colliders that keep the viewer inside the scene and smoothly return them to the center if they move out
  • Simple UI with basic weapon info and quick access to main presentation controls
